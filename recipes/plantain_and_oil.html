Plantain and oil


Description

This is a Cameroonian delicacy common th the North west region of the country

I personally learnt this dish from my father who originated from the North west of Cameroon.


Ingredients

This recipe requires very little ingredients. Namely;

  • Plantains (preferably semi ripe)
  • Palm oil (if possible that which is just from being processed)
  • some salt pepper and maggi

Steps

  1. Peel your plantains
  2. roast carefully on or over hot charcoal untill the blantains are all perfectly brown, its okay if some get blackish, youll just need a knife to scrape that off.
  3. After plantains are ready, pour some palm oil into a bowl (about 0.02l)
  4. introduce your plantains into thesame bowl, using a pistle gently pound your plantains for it to mix well with the oil
  5. add in saly, maggi and pepper to taste, Bonne Appetit!
